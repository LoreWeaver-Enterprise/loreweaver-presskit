Usage Guidelines

Do

  • Use the logo at a legible size with adequate clear space around it
  • Choose the logo variant that contrasts well with your background
  • Maintain the original proportions when resizing
  • Use "LoreWeaver" as one word with a capital L and capital W

Please avoid

  • Altering the logo colors outside the provided variants
  • Stretching, rotating, or distorting the logo
  • Placing the logo on busy backgrounds where it loses legibility
  • Adding effects such as drop shadows, gradients, or outlines to the logo
  • Writing the name as "Lore Weaver", "loreweaver", or "LOREWEAVER"
